Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese. Here is a simple recipe to make delicious lasagna at home.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add minced garlic and cook for another minute. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, dried basil, and a pinch of salt.
  5. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom. Layer with 3 lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, a layer of meat sauce, and a sprinkle of mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the top.
  6. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  7. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
